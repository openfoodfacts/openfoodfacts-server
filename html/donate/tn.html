Re thuse go duelela tekanyetsokabo ya Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng 2026!

Open Food Facts ke mahala 100% mme e ikemetse ka nosi ho indasteri ya dijo. Re tlhoka thuso ya lona go tswelela le go godisa porojeke.

Ke ka ntlha yang fa o neela Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng?

Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng ke "Wikipedia ya dijo", polokelotshedimosetso e kgolo ya mahala le e e bulegileng ya ya dikumo tsa dijo mo lefatsheng. Re simolotse Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng ka gonne re dumela gore tshedimosetso ka ga se re se jang ke ya kgatlhego ya setšhaba mme e tlhoka gore e nne ya setšhaba, e e gololesegileng le e e bulegileng.

Fa re ne re tlhoma Open Food Facts, re ne ra dira tlhopho e e tlhamaletseng ya go nna e e sa direng dipoelo gotlhelele go go oketsa tshusumetso ya rona ka go aba tiro ya rona yotlhe. We also made the clear choice that we would be totally independent from the industry, and thus to never accept any funding that could put our mission at risk.

Re tlhoka thuso ya lona go tswelela re dira le go godisa Open Food Dintlha. Tsweetswee akanyetsa go ntsha moneelo go sireletsa le go tshegetsa Dintlha tsa Dijo tse di Buletsweng.
