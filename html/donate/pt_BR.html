O Open Food Facts é a "Wikipedia dos alimentos", o maior banco de dados livre e aberto de produtos alimentícios do mundo. We started Open Food Facts because we believe information about what we eat is of public interest and needs to be public, free and open.

A priority

Food is a priority for health and ecology. Through transparency and education, Open Food Facts creates a virtuous circle between consumers and producers.

A common good

Our database is used by more than 100 mobile applications and for large scale scientific studies.

Accessible to everyone

Pionneer in giving public access to the Nutri-Score and now the Eco-score and personnalized scores, we make useful and quality information accessible to everyone.

Participative

Open Food Facts is co-constructed by its users. It ensures its independance and its resiliency, and guarantees the openness of data, software and processes.

Quando fundamos o Open Food Facts, fizemos a escolha clara de ser totalmente sem fins lucrativos para maximizar nosso impacto, doando todo o nosso trabalho. Também fizemos a escolha clara de que seríamos totalmente independentes da indústria, e, portando, nunca aceitar nenhum financiamento que poderia arriscar a nossa missão.
