Sisize sixhase isabelomali se-Open Food Facts 2026!

I-Open Food Facts imahhala engu-100% futhi izimele embonini yokudla. Sidinga usizo lwakho ukuze siqhubeke futhi sikhulise iphrojekthi.

Kungani unikela ukuze uvule amaqiniso okudla?

Open Food Facts is the "Wikipedia of food", the largest free and open database of food products in the world. Siqale i-Open Food Facts ngoba sikholelwa ukuthi ulwazi mayelana nokuthi esikudlayo lunentshisekelo yomphakathi futhi ludinga lube sesidlangalaleni, lukhululeke futhi luvuleleke.

Ngenkathi sisungula amaqiniso okudla okuvulekile, senze ukukhetha okucacile kokuthi ukungenzi inzuzo ngokuphelele ukuze sikhulise umthelela wethu ngokunikela ngawo wonke umsebenzi wethu. Siphinde senza ukukhetha okucacile ukuthi thina sizobe sizimele ngokuphelele embonini, futhi ngaleyo ndlela singalokothi samukele noma yiluphi uxhaso olungabeka umsebenzi wethu engozini.

Sidinga usizo lwakho ukuze siqhubeke nokusebenza futhi sikhulise Ukudla Okuvulekile Amaqiniso. Sicela ucabange ukwenza umnikelo ukuze uvikele futhi ugcine amaqiniso okudla okuvulekile.

Ngiyabonga,
Anca, Éloïse, Florence, Léonore, Marie, Christian, Ludovic, Sébastien, Vincent kanye nalo lonke ithimba le-Open Food Facts.

Lapho umnikelo wakho uya khona

Ubuchwepheshe: Ukuthuthukiswa, ukunakekelwa, amaseva ukuze kugcinwe isizindalwazi sikhula kanye nokwengeza izici ezintsha ezinhle kuhlelo lokusebenza leselula le-Open Food Facts kanye nesizindalwazi.

Abantu namaphrojekthi: Ukuze sinikeze usekelo lwethu olungcono kakhulu kumphakathi we-Open Ovulekile, ukusebenzisana nabacwaningi, nokwenza idatha yethu yemikhiqizo yokudla ibe nomthelela omkhulu empilweni yethu, iplanethi yethu, kanye nomphakathi.
