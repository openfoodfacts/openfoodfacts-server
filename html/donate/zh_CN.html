Open Food Facts 是“食物的维基百科”，是世界上最大的免费开放的 食品数据库。

We started Open Food Facts because we believe information about what we eat is information of public interest. 因此，它必须是 公开的、免费的和开放的。

Our data is used by citizens to make healthier food choices thanks to our mobile app, by public health researchers to study the impact of food on our health, by journalists for their reporting, and even by the food industry to improve their products. 这就是食物的力量 透明度：它造福于所有人！

When we founded Open Food Facts, we made the clear choice to be totally non-profit to maximize our impact by giving away all our work. 我们也明确做了选择，我们会完全独立于 从整个行业。所以，永远不会接受把任何资金 置于危险边缘。

因此，我们以小额资金开启了这个项目，现在，我们 预算还是很紧张 From 2012 to 2017 we were all volunteers and our annual budget was less than 1000 euros! 该项目已经增收了很多 但是，我们现在需要做更多事情才能完成自己的使命。

我们需要您的帮助才能继续运营 Open Food Facts。请考虑一下捐款，这样我们可以延续 Open Food Facts。
