Sincede sixhase ngemali i-Open Food Facts 2026!

I-Open Food Facts i-100% isimahla kwaye izimeleyo kushishino lokutya. Sifuna uncedo lwakho ukuze siqhubeke kwaye sikhulise iprojekthi.

Kutheni unikela ukuze uvule iinyaniso zokutya?

Iinyani ezivulekileyo zoKutya yeyona "Wikipedia yokutya", eyona datha inkulu yasimahla nevulekileyo yeemveliso zokutya emhlabeni. Siqale iiNyaniso eziVulelekileyo zoKutya kuba sikholelwa ukuba ulwazi malunga nento esiyityayo inomdla woluntu kwaye kufuneka ibe esidlangalaleni, ikhululeke kwaye ivuleleke.

Xa sasungula i-Open Food Facts, senze ukhetho olucacileyo ukuba ngokupheleleyo non-ngeniso ukuba ukwandisa impembelelo yethu ngokunikela yonke imisebenzi yethu. Kwakhona senze ukhetho olucacileyo ukuba thina siya kuba ngokupheleleyo ngokuzimeleyo kwishishini, kwaye ngaloo ndlela singaze samkele nayiphi na inkxaso-mali enokubeka umsebenzi wethu emngciphekweni.

Sidinga uncedo lwakho ukuze siqhubeke sisebenza kwaye sikhulise ukutya okuVulekileyo Iinyani. Nceda ucinge ngokwenza umnikelo ukuze ukhusele kwaye ugcine iiNyaniso eziVulekileyo zoKutya.

Enkosi,
Anca, Éloïse, Florence, Léonore, Marie, Christian, Ludovic, Sébastien, Vincent kunye nalo lonke iqela le-Open Food Facts.

Apho umnikelo wakho uya khona

Technology: Development, maintenance, servers to keep the database growing and to add cool new features to the Open Food Facts mobile app and web site.

Abantu kunye neeprojekthi: Ukubonelela ngeyona nkxaso ilungileyo kwi-Open Uluntu lwe-Food Facts, ukusebenzisana nabaphandi, kunye nokwenza idatha yemveliso yokutya inempembelelo enkulu kwimpilo yethu, iplanethi yethu, kunye noluntu.
