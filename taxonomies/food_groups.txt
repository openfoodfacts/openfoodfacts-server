# The food groups taxonomy is a hierarchy with up to 3 levels.
# Food groups are used by researchers (in particular the EREN team that created the Nutri-Score) for studies.
# Each product can be in only 1 of each food group at each level.

# The groups for each product are determined from its categories, thanks to properties in the categories taxonomy.

# The category taxonomy property "food_group:en: en:Fruits and vegetables" indicates a category is mapped to the food group en:Fruits and vegetables

# The 3 following entries are to be able to easily find products that don't have a food group entry at a given level

en:Unknown food group 2
