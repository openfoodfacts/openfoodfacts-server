Baza podatkov o živilskih izdelkih

Open Food Facts je odprta zbirka živilskih izdelkov s sestavinami, alergeni, podatki o hranilni vrednosti in vsemi informacijami, ki jih najdemo na nalepkah izdelkov.

Izdelalano od vseh

Open Food Facts so neprofitno združenje prostovoljcev.

15000 + sodelavcev, kot ste vi, ste dodali 1 000 000+ izdelkov iz 150 držav z uporabo naše Android , iPhone ali Windows Phone aplikacije ali njene kamere za skeniranje črtnih kod in prenos slik izdelkov in njihovih etiket.

Za vsakogar

Data about food is of public interest and has to be open. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Izboljšajte izbiro hrane

Knowledge is power. Food knowledge is power to eat better!

  • Dekodiranje nalepk

    Food additives E-numbers, allergens, packaging codes... Open Food Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels.

    Ocenjujemo tudi prehransko kakovost vsakega izdelka.

  • Poišči izdelke

    Naš obrazec za iskanje vključuje desetine kriterijev, tako da lahko najdete izdelke, ki ustrezajo vašim kriterijem.

  • Primerjaj in spremeni

    Do you know which breakfast cereals have the less fat and sugar? In 3 clicks you can create an interactive graph to show it. So you can change for healthier cereals, and by comparing products from different brands, you encourage producers to make them better.

  • Raziščite in odkrijte

    Curious about food? Explore products by facets like brands, categories, labels, origins of ingredients etc. Facets can be combined to discover new insights, for instance to map the origins of ingredients that bear a fair trade label.

Hrana za razmišljanje, inovacije in znanost

The Open Food Facts database is open data published under the Open Database Licence. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Znanost

Open Food Facts data is used by scientists in different fields (Nutrition, Biology, Linked Open Data...). And you don't need a PhD to take part in citizen science!

Prehranske stopnje

The French National Nutrition and Health Program uses Open Food Facts to validate the formula of its nutrition quality score and nutrition grades.
