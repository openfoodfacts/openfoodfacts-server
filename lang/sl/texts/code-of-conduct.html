Kodeks ravnanja

Open Food Facts is a project driven by a community of volunteers. Everyone is invited to participate, and to make sure everyone feels welcome at all times, we ask that you interact with kindness and courtesy with other members and that you follow those rules:

Pravila

Brez diskriminacije

Dejstva o odprtih živilih so namenjena zagotavljanju okolja brez vsakršnega vznemirjanja za vse, ne glede na starost, spol, spolno usmerjenost, invalidnost, fizični videz, raso ali religijo.

Ni nadlegovanja in nobenih groženj

Ne bomo tolerirali nadlegovanja udeležencev v kakršni koli obliki ali kar koli, kar ogroža osebno varnost.

Nobenih nespoštljivih zahtev posameznim sodelavcem

All Open Food Facts participants and contributors are unpaid volunteers who dedicate time and energy to the project. Please take this into account when you request something from an individual contributor, and do accept answers like "No," "Not now," "Maybe," and/or "I don't know". Repeated and/or undue pressure to make a contributor do/answer something will be considered as harassment.

Zahteve, ki bodo verjetno ustvarile precej dela, morajo biti poslane skrbnikom na naslov: contact@openfoodfacts.org in ne na posamezne sodelavce.

Posledice nespoštovanja kodeksa ravnanja

Osebe, ki kršijo ta pravila, so lahko začasno blokirane ali trajno odstranjene iz projekta po presoji skrbnikov združenja Open Food Facts.

Še posebej se bodo trajno odstranili slabi akterji, ki večkrat ignorirajo pravila in kulturo projekta, ki so nepotrebno argumentirani ali sovražni, ali ki so žaljivi in ne morejo sami popraviti svojega vedenja, ko jih drugi za to prosijo.

Poročanje o zlorabi

If you believe someone is harassing you or has otherwise violated this Code of conduct, please contact us at abuse@openfoodfacts.org to send us an abuse report. Please include as much detail as possible. It is easiest for us to address issues when we have more context.

Vaše poročilo bo prebral, obravnaval in nanj odgovarjal Stéphane Gigandet (predsednik Združenja Open Food Facts) in Florentin Raud (pobudnik tega kodeksa ravnanja).



Note: this code of conduct is inspired by the codes of conduct of other open communities like the Contributor Covenant. Feel free to reuse it or part of it.
