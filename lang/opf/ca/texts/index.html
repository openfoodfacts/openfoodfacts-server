Open Product Facts (Dades Reals i Obertes de Productes)

Open Products Facts està en construcció.

It is based on Open Food Facts and made by the same team. Estem en procés de fer canvis per tenir en compte els aspectes específics dels aliments per a mascotes. Join us on our Slack in the #openproductsfacts channel to discuss and invent Open Products Facts!

Descobrir

Open Products Facts is a general products (excepting food, cosmetics and pet food) database made by everyone, for everyone.

You can use it to make better choices about products, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

→ Learn more about Open Products Facts

Contribuir

Open Products Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. You can start contributing by adding a non-food, non petfood, non cosmetic product, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
