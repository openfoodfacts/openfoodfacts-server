On Saturday February 21st 2015 - Open Data Day - the free and open cosmetics products database Open Beauty Facts is launching Open Beauty Hunt, an operation to open cosmetics products data from as many different countries as possible.

1500 contributors have added 35000 products on Open Beauty Facts. The database includes products from more than 100 different countries, but the bulk of them comes from France - where Open Beauty Facts was launched in 2012 - and from a few countries like Spain, the UK, the US and Belgium where we have some very motivated contributors. Open Beauty Hunt aims to change that: we are going to add products from the 150 missing countries and try to reach a critical mass of products from as many countries as possible. Of even greater importance, Open Beauty Hunt aims to create and develop strong local communities of contributors.

Become an Open Beauty Explorer!

The easiest way to start to add products it to install our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone apps. You can also take pictures of products with your camera and upload them through the Open Beauty Facts web site.

Please also create a user account on the Open Beauty Facts web site, and then use it to login in the app so that we can attribute you all your contributions.

Become an Open Beauty Ambassador!

Tell your friends about Open Beauty Facts and ask them to contribute products from their countries!

You can claim the new contributors you recruit by sending us their Open Beauty Facts usernames to contact@openbeautyfacts.org or on Twitter using the #OpenBeautyHunt hashtag.
