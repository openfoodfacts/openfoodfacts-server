Prispevajte k Open Beauty Facts

Vse, kar potrebujete, je skeniranje! :-)

Ali lahko kozmetično industrijo naredimo bolj odprto in pregledno? Seveda, (skeniramo!) In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Vsakdo lahko prispeva

Bodite del naše skupne, brezplačne in odprte baze kozmetičnih izdelkov z vsega sveta!

Open Beauty Facts je neprofitni projekt, ki so ga v celoti izdelali prostovoljci, potrebujemo vas.

Dodajte izdelke

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Nimate pametnega telefona? Ni problema: s fotoaparatom lahko dodate tudi izdelke neposredno na spletno mesto.

Na spletni strani lahko izpolnite tudi informacije za izdelke, ki ste jih dodali vi ali drugi.

Povej svetu

Do you like Open Beauty Facts? Povej drugim o tem!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, share Open Beauty Facts on social media...

Predstavitev v živo! We have presented Open Beauty Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. Imamo veliko predstavitvenega gradiva (diapozitivi itd.), ki jih lahko prilagodite ali predstavite takšne, kot so.

Naj bo lokalno

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Skupaj lahko sodelujemo pri podpori in dodajanju logotipov za lokalne nalepke, za dekodiranje lokalnih kod pakiranja, tako da bomo lahko locirali izdelke itd.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Potrebna je strokovna pomoč

Developing Open Beauty Facts also requires specialized knowledge and expertise in many different areas:

  • Vodenje projektov

    Na voljo imamo veliko idej in verjetno jih imate vi še več, vendar je velik izziv katere določiti kot prednostne, sestaviti načrt in upravljati projekte, kadar je vsak udeleženec prostovoljec s pogosto omejenim časom.

  • Oblikovanje

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Beauty Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Razvoj

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS / Android / Windows Phone / FirefoxOS apps (currently developed in HTML and JS with Apache Cordova / Phonegap), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Gradnja skupnosti

    Zgraditi moramo lokalne skupnosti v vseh državah in jih hkrati združiti po vsem svetu.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Ste eden izmed njih?

  • Komunikacija

    Nismo prepričani, kaj bi napisali tukaj, bi lahko pomagali?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Posebni projekti

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Mogoče že imate ideje? Pomagajte nam, da jih spodbudimo naprej.

Pogovorimo se!

V naši skupnosti sodelavcev je veliko idej, energije in navdušenja, pridružite se nam!

Tu je nekaj mest, kjer lahko srečate druge sodelavce, se z njimi pogovarjate in sodelujete:

  • Slack

    Slack je najboljši način za druženje in sodelovanje z drugimi sodelavci, razvijalci in ponovnimi uporabniki. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Popolnoma se je spremenilo, kako sodelujemo. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Skupine Facebook

    We have Facebook groups for Open Beauty Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Beauty Facts and its sub-projects. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Beauty Facts, please join us on Slack and let's start the discussion!
