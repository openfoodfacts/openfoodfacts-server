A cosmetics products database

Open Beauty Facts is a database of cosmetics products with ingredients, allergens, indicia and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.

Izdelalano od vseh

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit association of volunteers.

1800+ contributors like you have added 13 000+ products from 134 countries using our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

Za vsakogar

Data about cosmetics is of public interest and has to be open. Celotna zbirka podatkov je objavljena kot odprti podatki in jih lahko uporabi kdorkoli in za kakršno koli uporabo. Oglejte si kul ponovne uporabe ali naredite svoje!

Make better cosmetics choices

Znanje je moč. Cosmetics knowledge is power to pamper yourself better!

  • Dekodiranje nalepk

    Cosmetics additives, F.I.L. codes, CI-numbers, allergens, packaging codes... Open Beauty Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels.

  • Poišči izdelke

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find products that match your criteria.

  • Primerjaj in spremeni

    Do you know which shampoos have the less chemicals? V treh klikih lahko ustvarite interaktivni graf za prikaz. So you can change for healthier shampoos, and by comparing products from different brands, you encourage producers to make them better.

  • Raziščite in odkrijte

    Curious about cosmetics? Raziščite izdelke po vidikih, kot so blagovne znamke, kategorije, nalepk, poreklo sestavin itd. Fasete je mogoče kombinirati za odkrivanje novih spoznanj, na primer za preslikavo izvora sestavin, ki nosijo oznako poštene trgovine.

Cosmetics, for innovation and science

Naše podatke lahko ponovno uporabimo za izobraževanje, nove izdelke in storitve, raziskave in drugo!

The Open Beauty Facts database is open data published under the Open Database Licence. Vsakdo jo lahko uporabi za kakršne koli namene - komercialne ali nekomercialne - pod pogojem, da je vir podatkov dodeljen in se izboljšajo baze podatkov ter se delijo na podoben način.

Izobraževanje

How many cosmetics commercials have you seen in your life? Odprti podatki lahko pomagajo videti dejstva pod trženjem.

Izdelki in storitve

Open Beauty Facts data can be reused in both non-commercial and commercial projects, as long as improvements to the database are shared as well.

Znanost

Open Beauty Facts data is used by scientists in different fields (Chemistry, Biology, Linked Open Data...). Ne potrebujete doktorata, da bi sodelovali v državljanski znanosti!
