Open Beauty Hunt

A global quest to open cosmetics data from all over the world.

Español - Français - Italiano


צויר על ידי פרנסואה לקומט

On Saturday February 21st 2019 - Open Data Day - the free and open cosmetics products database Open Beauty Facts is launching Open Beauty Hunt, an operation to open cosmetics products data from as many different countries as possible.

500 מתנדבים הוסיפו 9000 מוצרים ל־Open Beauty Facts. The database includes products from more than 100 different countries, but the bulk of them comes from France - where Open Beauty Facts was launched in 2012 - and from a few countries like Spain, the UK, the US and Belgium where we have some very motivated contributors. Open Beauty Hunt aims to change that: we are going to add products from the 150 missing countries and try to reach a critical mass of products from as many countries as possible. Of even greater importance, Open Beauty Hunt aims to create and develop strong local communities of contributors.

יום הנתונים הפתוחים


Beauty is a great way to "show and tell" people about open data

"Open Data Day is a gathering of citizens in cities around the world to write applications, liberate data, create visualizations and publish analyses using open public data to show support for and encourage the adoption open data policies by the world's local, regional and national governments."

בעוד שרוב הנתונים הפתוחים מגיעים מגופים ממשלתיים וציבוריים, חלקים חשובים מאוד מהמידע מגיעים מהקהילה - באיסוף על ידי עצמאיים - כמו הנתונים שמשמשים ליצירת OpenStreetMap. Beauty products data in Open Beauty Facts is aso crowdsourced by contributors who use their smartphones to scan product barcodes and to take pictures of products, ingredients lists and nutrition facts.

Cosmetics is something every human being is interested in, and open cosmetics data can be a very good introduction to open data, as it is useful to everyone (e.g. to decode labels, to compare products, to eat better, and to play !). We hope Open Beauty Facts and Open Beauty Hunt will be presented in many Open Data Day gatherings. אם יש לך תכנית לארגן יום נתונים פתוחים בעיר שלך יש לנו מגוון מצגות זמינות ונשמח מאוד לערוך אתך החלפות, אז נא ליצור קשר!

Open Beauty Explorers and Open Beauty Ambassadors

Starting on Open Data Day and for one week, Open Beauty Facts contributors will become Open Beauty Explorers: their goal will be to add as many products from their country on Open Beauty Facts and to make it reach the next level: from no product to one product, from 1 to 10, 10 to 100, 100 to 1000, 1000 to 10000... (and for people in France we have a special goal to go from 25000 to 100000!).

Existing and new contributors will also be invited to become Open Beauty Ambassadors who recruit new contributors, both in their country and outside their country. In particular ambassadors are highly encouraged to present Open Beauty Facts to their family and friends living in countries where we currently have no or few products.

התחרות הידידותית Open Beauty Hunt

From Saturday February 21st to Sunday March 1st, we will keep track of the top Open Beauty Explorers and Open Beauty Ambassadors.

Open Beauty Explorers will be awarded explorer points for each new product they add:

  • 100 points for the 1st product added to a country (i.e. there are currently no products in Open Beauty Facts for that country)
  • 20 נקודות על כל אחד מ־10 המוצרים הראשונים שנוספים במדינה
  • 10 נקודות על כל אחד מ־100 המוצרים הראשונים
  • 5 נקודות על כל אחד מ־1000 המוצרים הראשונים
  • 2 נקודות על כל אחד מ־10000 המוצרים הראשונים
  • נקודה אחת על כל מוצר לאחר מכן

הנקודות מוכפלות כאשר עמודי המוצרים מושלמים: עם תמונות רכיבים ועובדות תזונתיות וכשכל הנתונים נוספו.

"Open your fridge!" suggests this magnet :-)

Open Beauty Ambassadors will also receive ambassador points corresponding to the explorer points of all the new contributors they recruit.

All Open Beauty Explorers and Ambassadors will receive an Open Beauty Facts magnet and sticker and the top Explorers and Ambassadors will receive some small rewards (TBD).

We will also keep track of the total of points for each country.

Become an Open Beauty Explorer!

The easiest way to start to add products it to install our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone apps. You can also take pictures of products with your camera and upload them through the Open Beauty Facts web site.

Please also create a user account on the Open Beauty Facts web site, and then use it to login in the app so that we can attribute you all your contributions.

Become an Open Beauty Ambassador!

Tell your friends about Open Beauty Facts and ask them to contribute products from their countries!

You can claim the new contributors you recruit by sending us their Open Beauty Facts usernames to contact@openbeautyfacts.org or on Twitter using the #OpenBeautyHunt hashtag.

Present Open Beauty Facts and the Open Beauty Hunt during Open Data Day

If you are organizing or participating in a local Open Data Day event (see the world map of planned events), we would be very grateful if you could present the Open Beauty Facts project and the Open Beauty Hunt event.
