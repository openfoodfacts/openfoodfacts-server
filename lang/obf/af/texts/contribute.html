Dra by tot Open Beauty Facts

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

Kan ons die skoonheidsmiddelsektor oper en meer deursigtig maak? Yes we scan! In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Everyone can contribute

Maak deel uit van ons gesamentlike, gratis en oop databasis van kosmetiese produkte van regoor die wêreld!

Open Beauty Facts is 'n nie-winsgewende projek wat uitsluitlik deur vrywilligers aangebied word. Ons het jou wel nodig.

Add products

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

On the web site you can also fill in the information for products you added or others added.

Tell the world

Hou jy van Open Beauty Facts? Tell others about it!

Jy kan die projek aan jou familie en vriende voorlê, wys hulle hoe om die program te installeer en by te dra, skryf 'n blogpos, deel Open Beauty Facts op sosiale media...

And present it live! Ons het Open Beauty feite aan baie verskillende gehore in baie verskillende instellings voorgelê, en ons kry altyd 'n baie entoesiastiese reaksie wanneer ons wys wat gedoen kan word wanneer die data vir produkte oop is. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

U kan help om die webwerf en mobiele program te vertaal na jou taal, en om aanbiedings, aankondigings, ens. te vertaal.

We can work together to support and add logos for local labels, to decode local packager codes so that we can map products etc.

Begin of sluit aan by 'n plaaslike bydraers gemeenskap: voeg plaaslike produkte by, werf vriende, bied die projek aan in plaaslike ontmoetings en konferensies ens.

Expert help needed

Die ontwikkeling van Open Beauty Facts vereis ook gespesialiseerde kennis en kundigheid op baie verskillende gebiede:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Ons het hulp nodig om 'n beter gebruikers ervaring op die Open Beauty feite webwerf en foon te bou, hul ontwerp te verbeter, om treffende aanbieding materiaal te skep ens.

  • Development

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS and Android apps (currently Apache Cordova / Phonegap, with a native Android version almost ready and much work to do on iOS), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub (Android, iOS, Cordova.)

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Facebook groups

    We have Facebook groups for Open Beauty Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    Ons het ook 'n wiki wat ons saam gebruik om Open Beauty Facts en sy subprojekte te dokumenteer. Ons gebruik ook vir wiki om vertalings te bestuur en om te werk aan die taksonomieë / hiërargieë van kategorieë, etikette ens.

Daar is baie meer maniere om by te dra tot Open Beauty Facts, asseblief sluit by ons aan op Slack en laat ons die bespreking begin!
