Contribute to Open Beauty Facts

Apa yang anda perlu buat adalah sikap mesra-imbas! :-)

Can we make the cosmetics industry more open and transparent? Ya kita boleh imbas! In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Sesiapa sahaja boleh memberi sumbangan

Be part of our collaborative, free and open database of cosmetic products from around the world!

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit project made entirely by volunteers, we do need you.

Tambah produk

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Tiada telefon pintar? Tiada masalah: anda masih boleh menggunakan kamera anda untuk menambah produk terus ke dalam tapak sesawang kami.

Di laman web kami, anda juga boleh isikan maklumat untuk produk yang anda tambah atau untuk produk yand sudah ditambah oleh orang lain.

Sebarkan kepada seluruh dunia

Do you like Open Beauty Facts? Beritahu orang lain tentangnya!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, share Open Beauty Facts on social media...

Dan bentangkannya secara langsung! We have presented Open Beauty Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. Kami mempunyai banyak bahan-bahan pembentangan (slaid dan sebagainya) yang anda boleh suaikan atau bentangkan sebegitu.

Jadikanya setempat

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Kita boleh bekerjasama untuk menyokong dan menambah logo label tempatan, menyahkod kod pembunhkus tempatan untuk memetakan produk dan sebagainya.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Bantuan pakar diperlukan

Developing Open Beauty Facts also requires specialized knowledge and expertise in many different areas:

  • Pengurusan projek

    Kami ada banyak idea dan kamu mungkin ada lebih banyak lagi, tetapi agak mencabar untuk mengutamakannya, untuk membina hala tuju, dan menguruskan projek apabila setiap peserta adalah sukarelawan dengan masa yang terhad.

  • Reka bentuk

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Beauty Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Pembangunan

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS and Android apps (currently Apache Cordova / Phonegap, with a native Android version almost ready and much work to do on iOS), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub (Android, iOS, Cordova.)

  • Pembinaan komuniti

    Kita perlu membina komuniti-komuniti setempat di seluruh negara dan pada masa yang sama dapat menyatukan mereka secara sejagat.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Adakah anda salah seorang daripada mereka?

  • Komunikasi

    Kami tidak pasti apa yang perlu ditulis di sini, anda boleh membantu?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Projek khas

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Anda ada idea bernas? Jika ada, bantulah kami dengan mengutarakan idea tersebut.

Jom kita bincang!

Ada banyak idea, tenaga dan semangat yang telah dikongsi bersama oleh para penyumbang komuniti kami, jom sertailah kami!

Berikut merupakan beberapa tempat, anda boleh bertemu dengan para penyumbang, berkomunikasi dan bekerjasama dengan mereka:

  • Slack

    Slack adalah cara terbaik untuk berinteraksi dan bekerjasama dengan penyumbang, pembangun, dan pengguna lain. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Ia sepenuhnya telah mengubah cara kita bekerjasama. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Kumpulan Facebook

    We have Facebook groups for Open Beauty Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Beauty Facts and its sub-projects. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Beauty Facts, please join us on Slack (in the #openbeautyfacts channel) and let's start the discussion!
