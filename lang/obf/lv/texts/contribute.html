Contribute to Open Beauty Facts

Viss, kas Jums nepieciešams, ir skenēšanas prasme! :-)

Can we make the cosmetics industry more open and transparent? Jā, skenējot! In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Ikviens var piedalíties

Be part of our collaborative, free and open database of cosmetic products from around the world!

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit project made entirely by volunteers, we do need you.

Pievienot produktu

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Nav viedtālruņa? Nav problēmu: varat arī izmantot savu kameru, lai produktus tieši pievienotu tīmekļa vietnei.

Tīmekļa vietnē jūs varat arī aizpildīt informāciju par pievienotajiem vai citiem pievienotajiem produktiem.

Pastāsti pasaulei

Do you like Open Beauty Facts? Pastāstiet citiem par to!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, share Open Beauty Facts on social media...

Piedāvājiet to dzīvajá! We have presented Open Beauty Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. Mums ir daudz prezentācijas materiālu (slaidi utt.), kurus jūs varat pielāgot vai prezentēt kā tādu.

Padariet to lokālu

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Mēs varam strādāt kopā, lai atbalstītu un pievienotu logotipus vietējām etiķetēm, lai dekodētu vietējos iepakojuma kodus, lai mēs varētu mapēt produktus utt.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Ekspertu palīdzība nepieciešama

Developing Open Beauty Facts also requires specialized knowledge and expertise in many different areas:

  • Projektu vadība

    Mums ir ideju idejas, un jums, iespējams, ir vēl vairāk, taču tas ir diezgan izaicinājums, lai tos prioritētu, veidotu ceļvedi un pārvaldītu projektus, ja katrs dalībnieks ir brīvprātīgais, kuram bieži vien ir ierobežots laiks.

  • Dizains

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Beauty Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Izstrāde

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS / Android / Windows Phone / FirefoxOS apps (currently developed in HTML and JS with Apache Cordova / Phonegap), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Kopienas veidosana

    Mums ir jāveido vietējās kopienas visās valstīs un vienlaikus jāapvieno tās visā pasaulē.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Vai tu esi viens no viņiem?

  • Komunikācija

    Mēs neesam pārliecināti, ko rakstīt šeit, vai jūs varētu palīdzēt?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular we need help for public and media relations.

  • Īpaši projekti

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Varbūt jums jau ir idejas? Ja tádas ir, lūdzu, palīdziet mums to virzīt uz priekšu.

Parunāsim!

Mūsu līdzstrādnieku kopienā ir kopīgas idejas, enerģija un entuziasms, pievienojieties mums!

Šeit ir dažas vietnes, kurās varat satikt citus dalībniekus, runāt ar viņiem un strādāt ar viņiem:

  • Slack

    Slack ir labākais veids, kā mijiedarboties un sadarboties ar citiem ieguldītājiem, izstrādātājiem un atkārtotās izmantošanas veidiem. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Tas pilnīgi mainīja to, kā mēs strādājam kopā. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Facebook grupas

    We have Facebook groups for Open Beauty Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Vikivietne

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Beauty Facts and its sub-projects. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Beauty Facts, please join us on Slack and let's start the discussion!
