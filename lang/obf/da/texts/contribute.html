Contribute to Open Beauty Facts

Alt du behøver er et skan gåpå mod! :-)

Can we make the cosmetics industry more open and transparent? Yes we scan! In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Alle kan bidrage

Be part of our collaborative, free and open database of cosmetic products from around the world!

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit project made entirely by volunteers, we do need you.

Tilføj produkter

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Ingen smartphone? Intet problem: Du kan også bruge dit kamera til at tilføje produkter direkte på webstedet.

På webstedet kan du også angive oplysninger for de produkter, som du - eller andre - har tilføjet.

Fortæl verden

Do you like Open Beauty Facts? Fortæl andre om det!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, share Open Beauty Facts on social media...

Og præsentere det live! We have presented Open Beauty Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. Vi har masser af præsentationsmaterialer (dias mm.), som du kan tilpasse eller præsentere som de er.

Gør det lokaliseret

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Vi kan arbejde sammen for at understøtte og tilføje logoer for lokale etiketter, for at afkode lokale pakkekoder, så vi kan kortlægge produkter mv.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Eksperthjælp søges

Developing Open Beauty Facts also requires specialized knowledge and expertise in many different areas:

  • Projektstyring

    Vi har masser af ideér, og du har nok endnu flere, men det er en udfordring at prioritere dem, lægge slagplaner og at styre projekter, når alle deltager er frivillige med ofte begrænset tid til rådighed.

  • Design

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Beauty Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Udvikling

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS / Android / Windows Phone / FirefoxOS apps (currently developed in HTML and JS with Apache Cordova / Phonegap), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Fællesskabsopbygning

    Vi skal opbygge lokalfællesskaber i alle lande og samtidig forene dem globalt.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Er du en af ​​dem?

  • Kommunikation

    Vi er ikke sikre på, hvad der skal skrives her, kan du hjælpe?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular we need help for public and media relations.

  • Specialprojekter

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Du har måske allerede ideér? Hvis du har, kan du hjælpe os med at skubbe dem fremad.

Lad os snakke sammen!

Mange ideér, energi og entusiasme deles i vores bidragsyderfællesskaber, tilmeld dig!

Her er nogle steder, hvor du kan møde andre bidragsydere, tale og samarbejde med dem:

  • Slack

    Slack er den bedste måde til at interagere og samarbejde med andre bidragydere, udviklere og "genbrugere". It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Det ændrede fuldstændigt, hvordan vi samarbejdede. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Facebook-grupper

    We have Facebook groups for Open Beauty Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Beauty Facts and its sub-projects. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Beauty Facts, please join us on Slack and let's start the discussion!
