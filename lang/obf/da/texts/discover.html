En kosmetikproduktdatabase

Open Beauty Facts er en kosmetikproduktdatabase med ingredienser, allergener, kendetegn og alle øvrige informationer, som fremgår af produktetiketterne.

Gerereret af alle

Open Beauty Facts er en nonprofit sammenslutning af frivillige.

1800+ bidragsydere, som dig, har tilføjet 13.000+ produkter fra 134 lande vha. Android-,iPhone- ellerWindows Phone-apperne eller deres kameraer til skanning af stregkoder og upload af produkterfotos og -etiketter.

For alle

Data about cosmetics is of public interest and has to be open. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Foretag bedre kosmetikvalg

Knowledge is power. Cosmetics knowledge is power to pamper yourself better!

  • Afkode etiketter

    Kosmetiktilsætningsstoffer, F.I.L. koder, CI-numre, allergener, emballagekoder... Open Beauty Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels.

  • Find produkter

    Søgeformularen indeholder snesevis af kriterier, du kan matche produkter op imod.

  • Sammenlign og ændre

    Do you know which shampoos have the less chemicals? In 3 clicks you can create an interactive graph to show it. So you can change for healthier shampoos, and by comparing products from different brands, you encourage producers to make them better.

  • Udforsk og opdag

    Curious about cosmetics? Explore products by facets like brands, categories, labels, origins of ingredients etc. Facets can be combined to discover new insights, for instance to map the origins of ingredients that bear a fair trade label.

Kosmetik, innovation og videnskab

Vores data kan genbruges til uddannelse, nye produkter og tjenester, forskning og mere!

The Open Beauty Facts database is open data published under the Open Database Licence. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Uddannelse

How many cosmetics commercials have you seen in your life? Open data can help to see the facts under the marketing.

Produkter og salgskanaler

Open Beauty Facts-data kan genbruges i både ikke-kommercielle og kommercielle projekter, så længe forbedringer til databasen ligeledes deles.

Videnskab

Open Beauty Facts data is used by scientists in different fields (Chemistry, Biology, Linked Open Data...). And you don't need a PhD to take part in citizen science!
