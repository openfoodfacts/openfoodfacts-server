Haske na zirga-zirgar ababen hawa

Tsarin hasken wuta (mai launin ja, lemo, kore) yana sanya damar gano yawan kitsen mai, mai cike da kitse, sukari da gishiri a cikin kayan abinci.

Hukumar Kula da Kayan Aikin Abinci ta Amurka (FSA) ce ta kafa wannan tsarin na zirga-zirgar ababen hawa. Wasu masana'antun suna amfani da shi da son rai a Burtaniya, amma Hukumar Turai ta ki shi a shekara ta 2010.

A Batun bude kayayyaki ns Abinci, lokacin da aka san mahimmancin abinci mai gina jiki, ana nuna fitilun zirga-zirgar ababen hawa a cikin shafukan samfuran.

An bayyana tsarin lissafin da ke bayyana launuka na hasken a shafin shafi na hasken wuta mai launi uku - umarnin (Faransanci) na Kungiyar Hadin Kan abinci.
