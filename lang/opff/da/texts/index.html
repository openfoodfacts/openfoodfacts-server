Open Pet Food Facts

Bemærk: Open Pet Facts er under opførelse.

It is based on Open Food Facts and made by the same team. We are in the process of making changes to take into account the specific aspects of pet food. Join us on our Slack in the #openpetfoodfacts channel to discuss and invent Open Beauty Facts!

Opdag

Open Pet Food Facts er en dyrefoderproduktdatabase udarbejdet af alle, for alle.

Du kan benytte den til at foretage bedre valg af dyrefoder, og da det er åbne data, kan enhver genbruge dem til ethvert formål.

Bidrag

Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. You can start contributing by adding a petfood product, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
