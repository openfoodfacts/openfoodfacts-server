Kontakt Open Food Facts-teamet

Har du et spørgsmål, en kommentar eller et forslag til Open Food Facts, kan du sende en e-mail via contact@openfoodfacts.org .

Please note: Open Food Facts is a collaborative database of food products, we do not sell any product and we do not have links to their producers. For a question or complain about a product, you need to write directly to the producer (address on the product label).

Du kan også få fat i os via Twitter: @OpenFoodFacts

Og chatte med Open Food Facts-medlemmer og -bidragsydere via Slack.
