Hvordan fungerer dette spil?

Spillet indlæser et tilfældigt produkt fra Open Food Facts, der behøver færdiggørelse. Brugerfladen er enkel og lader dig interagere ved at besvare et spørgsmål om en egenskab for produktet via "Ja", "Oversspring" eller "Nej" samt om nødvendigt få yderligere produktinformation.

Brugerfladen er opdelt i 3 elementer:


1. Spørgsmålet

For at besvare spørgsmålet klikkes blot på én af de 3 muligheder (Ja/Nej/Overspring), hvorefter spillet automatisk viser næste produkt.

At the top of the screen are 4 buttons (filters) allowing you to select the type of questions you wish to answer:

  • label: Whether the brand has a specific label such as "Organic" or "Fair trade"
  • category: Whether the product belongs to a specific category such as "Yogurts" or "Chocolate cookies"
  • brand: Questions relating to the brand of the product
  • product_weight: Questions relating to the weight of the product (eg 100 g, 1L, etc.)

You can see below:

  1. A search field, allowing you to specify which brand, label, category you want to work on,
  2. The question to be answered, such as "Does this product correspond to the indicated value?"
  3. a photo of the product,
  4. 3 buttons to answer the question.

When you are not 100% sure of your answer you must answer "Skip"!Above all, do not answer "Yes" or "No" if you are not sure of your choice. Another person will take care of answering this question later. This game is above all collaborative and aims to contribute efficiently and qualitatively to Open Food Facts data.

2. Access information

If the answer is not visible on the product image, this part of the screen allows you to view the product information. You can click on:

  • Product photos you can zoom on,
  • The "View" button: provides access to the product page on the Web version of Open Food Facts,
  • The "Edit" button: lets you edit the product on Open Food Facts,.

This last option is useful when the product requires further modification.

3. Annotations

On the far right of the screen (or at the very bottom of the screen if you are on mobile), there are annotations that let you know:

  • The number of products remaining to be treated (varies depending on the filters applied),
  • The number of annotated products,
  • A history of the latest actions taken during your session.

If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us on the Slack or on the Facebook group.
