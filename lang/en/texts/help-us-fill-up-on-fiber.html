Help us fill up on fiber

The mission

The mission consists in adding the content value for fiber to the Open Food Facts product sheets in order to be able to compute the Nutri-Score.

We have designed algorithms which make it possible to identify the products for which we have received a photo which contains a nutritional table with a line for fibers, but for which the value for fibers has not been entered in our database.

It is necessary to edit the files of these products to select the new photo of the nutritional table (if necessary), and enter the missing value for the fibers.

Why this is important

This will allow this fiber value to be taken into account instantly for the computation of the Nutri-Score on the Open Food Facts site and the Open Food Facts app.

Actions to accomplish

To carry out the mission, you must first create an account on the Open Food Facts website (if you have installed our mobile application, you may also have created an account that also works on the website) and log in the site.

In the "Teams" field, indicate that you are part of your favorite team. (and you can be part of 2 other teams, for example with your friends, your colleagues, or be part of a regional team).

Join us on our Slack discussion space for help, discuss with other contributors and if you wish to participate in other team missions!

Then:

  1. Select a product from the list of products for which the fiber content must be entered.
  2. Click on Edit page at the top of the product page, and:
  • go down to the nutritional information section
  • select and crop the nutritional table from the photos we have for the product, choosing the most recent photo possible. (unless the photo selected for the nutritional table is already the most recent)
  • enter the fiber content
  • save the product page

Profiles sought

This mission is for all people who have a little time (10 minutes to install and discover the Open Food Facts site and the product editing form, and a few minutes for each product) and who want to participate in a common good, free and open, which improves nutrition and health for all !

To participate, you only need a computer and an Internet connection.
