Install the Open Food Facts mobile app

To scan food products, get their Nutri-Score, their NOVA group to know if it's an ultra-processed food, and to decypher food additives, install the free Open Food Facts app!



You can also add new products to Open Food Facts and build a common good to improve nutrition and health for everybody. Thank you !

If you use the app and enjoy the Open Food Facts project, it would be nice to leave a review on the App Store or the Play Store to encourage others to install it. Thank you!
