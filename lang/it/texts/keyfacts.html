Open Foods Facts è un database di informazioni sugli alimenti unico al mondo:

  • collaborativo : paragonabile a Wikipedia, ha 16.000 collaboratori
  • international and no limit to size and products: born in France, it now contains 700,000 products in more than 180 countries - 16 countries have more than 1,000 products, 8 more than 8,000
  • freely reusable (open data): dozens of companies, researchers, innovators freely reuse the data for their own use
  • strettamente indipendente del settore agroalimentare, è gestito da un'associazione di diritto francese, legge del 1901.

With 100,000 users / day, Open Food Facts has already become, in 6 years only, the reference for food information in several countries. It supports research and public policies aimed at a healthier diet.

Le 5 principali storie di successo di Open Food Facts

Creating the first freely reusable global database on food products
More than 700,000 products identified by more than 16,000 contributors. The Open Food Facts data is also published on the official French open data portal, data.gouv.fr.

Informing the general public and professionals
The consumer app is installed on more than 750,000 phones or tablets. Il sito e l'applicazione sono usati da 100.000 utenti / giorno e 6 milioni di utenti all'anno.

Promoting nutritional quality indicators
For 3 years, Open Food Facts has been supporting the roll out of Nutri-Score in 5 European countries. In France, Open Food Facts has been displaying the Nutri-Score since 2015, before its officialization. In Spain and Belgium, Open Food Facts also precedes the adoption of Nutri-Score by these countries. Open Food Facts also includes other indicators such as the NOVA classification.

Creating and maintaining an ecosystem of uses and research
Since its creation, Open Food Facts has developed a real ecosystem of reuse of its data. Since the Open Food Facts data is freely reusable, several information sites, start-ups, research laboratories, teachers, etc., have started their activity and / or use the Open Food Facts data daily: Yuka, Foodvisor, Scaneat, Ya Quoi Dedans? (What’s inside?) (Super U), Buy Or Not, nopalm.org, My Nutritional Info, ...

Change the practices of manufacturers
For 2 years, Open Food Facts has helped to change the practices of the food industry; they know and rely on Open Food Facts to develop their products. Molte persone comunicano spontaneamente i dati dei loro prodotti sulla piattaforma: Fleury Michon, Super U, Carrefour, Casino, ecc.

Chi utilizza i dati di Open Food Facts?

Sarebbe troppo lungo nominare tutte le organizzazioni che si basano sulla piattaforma Open Food Facts.

  • Più di 100 applicazioni mobile che soddisfano le esigenze di vari pubblici: diabete (VeryDiab), allergie, equilibrio alimentare tramite intelligenza artificiale (FoodVisor), disabilità (Digit-Eyes) e altro ancora. La nostra base ha anche permesso la creazione di Yuka.
  • Molti siti Web: CombiendeSucres.fr, CestEmballePresDeChezVous.fr ...
  • Data-journalists: Decoders of the World, BFM
  • Ricercatori in quasi 100 articoli scientifici
  • Food banks and solidarity stores : Data For Good project in the continuity of 'a pilot initiated by the Red Cross, integration into the cash management of a Toulouse cooperative.
  • French industry flagships: Terraillon to bring its connected scales to life.
  • Programming schools (Simplon, Open Classrooms) have integrated Open Food Facts as a motivating project in their curriculum.
